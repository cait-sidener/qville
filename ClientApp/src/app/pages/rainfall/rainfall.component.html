Rainfall Intensity Values for Kansas Counties

The 1997 Rain Intesities Data comes from the Technical Memorandum NWS HYDRO-35 (for durations of 5 minutes to 60 minutes) and Technical Paper No 40 (TP-40) of the US Weather Bureau (longer durations)

Methods making use of the 1997 data:

  • K-TRAN KU-06-4 Extended Rational
  • K-TRAN KU-06-4 3 Var Regression
  • Rational Method

Methods making use of the 2013 data:

  • FHWA-KS-15-12

This is for information only. The program will look up the values when calculating. The program assumes time of concentration to be equal to duration and the intensity values are linear interpolated for durations between the table durations.
